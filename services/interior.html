Interior Design

I believe that interior design is just as important as exterior design. The interior of a building should be functional, visually pleasing, and reflective of the people who use it.

❮ ❯

When you work with an architect for your exterior design, you can expect a range of services that include:

  • Space planning: An architect will work with you to determine how the interior space should be arranged to meet your needs. This includes considering factors such as traffic flow, furniture placement, and storage.
  • Material and finishes selection: An architect can help you select materials and finishes that are both visually appealing and functional. This includes flooring, wall coverings, and lighting.
  • Custom furniture design: If you need custom furniture for your space, an architect can design pieces that are tailored to your specific needs and preferences.
  • Lighting design: Lighting is a critical aspect of any interior design, and an architect can create a lighting plan that enhances the functionality and ambiance of your space.
  • Construction administration: An architect can oversee the construction process to ensure that the design is being implemented correctly and that any issues are addressed promptly.

In addition to these core services, architects can also provide expertise in areas such as sustainability, acoustics, and code compliance. They can help to create a design that is energy-efficient, acoustically comfortable, and meets local building codes.
