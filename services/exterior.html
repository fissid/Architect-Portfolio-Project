Exterior Design

Exterior design is a crucial aspect of any building project, and it's an area where architects can make a significant impact. At an architecture, I specialize in creating visually stunning and functional exterior designs that meet the unique needs of my clients.

When you work with an architect for your exterior design, you can expect a range of services that include:

  • Site analysis: Before any design work begins, an architect will conduct a site analysis to evaluate the existing conditions and opportunities of your property. This will help to inform the design and ensure that it is appropriate for the site.
  • Conceptual design: After the site analysis, the architect will develop a conceptual design that outlines the overall look and feel of the exterior. This will include considerations such as materials, colors, and textures.
  • Design development: Once the conceptual design is approved, the architect will work on the design development phase. This involves refining the design and creating detailed drawings and specifications that will guide the construction process.
  • Construction administration: An architect will also oversee the construction process to ensure that the design is being implemented correctly and that any issues are addressed promptly.

In addition to these core services, architects can also provide expertise in areas such as sustainability, accessibility, and code compliance. They can help to create a design that is energy-efficient, meets local building codes, and provides easy access for people with disabilities.
